Frequently Asked Questions

What is your hourly rate?

I offer project-based pricing, but hourly rates typically range from $25-$75 depending on project complexity and your specific requirements.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary. A simple website takes 2-3 weeks, while complex applications may take 2-3 months. I provide detailed timelines after understanding requirements.

Do you provide maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer post-launch support packages including bug fixes, updates, and technical assistance.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Java, SQL, Node.js, React, and various backend frameworks.

Can you work with existing code?

Absolutely! I can debug, optimize, and extend existing projects. Code quality is always a priority.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, I can sign NDAs and confidentiality agreements for sensitive projects.

How do you handle project communication?

I provide regular updates via email, messaging apps, or video calls based on your preference.

What's your refund policy?

If you're unsatisfied with the deliverables, we work together to make revisions. Terms are discussed upfront.

Do you host websites?

I don't host directly, but I guide you through deployment options like Netlify, Vercel, AWS, and traditional hosting.

Can you optimize existing websites?

Yes, I offer SEO optimization, performance improvements, and modernization of existing websites.
